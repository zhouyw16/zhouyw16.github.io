People

Xin Wang is currently an Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University.

He got both of his Ph.D. and B.E. degrees in Computer Science and Technology from Zhejiang University, China. He also holds a Ph.D. degree in Computing Science from Simon Fraser University, Canada.

His research interests include cross-modal multimedia intelligence and recommendation in social media. He has published several high-quality research papers in top conferences including ICML, KDD, WWW, MM, SIGIR, AAAI, IJCAI, and CIKM. He is the recipient of 2017 China Postdoctoral innovative talents supporting program.
