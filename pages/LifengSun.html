People

Lifeng Sun is currently a Professor at the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University.

He got his B.E. degrees in System Engineering, National University of Defense Technology, Changsha, China and his Ph.D. in System Engineering, National University of Defense Technology, Changsha, China.

His professional interests lie in the areas of Video Streaming, Video Analysis and Processing, Multimedia Big Data, Social Media, Multimedia Cloud Computing. Recently, he is working on Multimedia Edge Computing. He has published over 120 papers in the above domain including IEEE JSAC, TMM, TPDS, TIP, TCSVT, TMC, CVPR, INFOCOM, ICME, ACM TOMM, Multimedia, SIGIR, AAAI, WWW. He got the Annual Best Paper Award of IEEE TCSVT (2010), Best Paper Award of ACM Multimedia (2012), Best Student Paper Award of Multimedia Modeling (2015) and Best Student Paper finalists of IEEE ICASSP(2008) and MMSP(2007). He has led many collaborative research projects with industry, including Intel, Microsoft, Samsung, Boeing, Tencent, Baidu, Alibaba, Huawei, Kuaishou etc.
